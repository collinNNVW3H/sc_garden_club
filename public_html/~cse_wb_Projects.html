Current Projects

We have a very exciting fall planned for this school year.

Thus far we have planted rutabagas. Later on this semester we

will grow Heliopolis, beauty berries, heather and pansies.

We will be holding a poll sometime later this semester where

we will allow students of Simpson College to vote on next

semester's plants.

Past Projects

Last year was quite busy, but very successful. We were able to grow

four vegetables and five flowers during the school year. In the fall

we grew turnips, sedum, chrysanthemums and for Halloween, pumpkins.

Spring was just as fun. We were able to plant and grow avocados, radishes,

peonies, pink tulips and primroses.
