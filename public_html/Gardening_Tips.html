Gardening Tips

The following are the Simpson Gardening Clubs top 10 beginner gardening tips!

  • Create a gardeners portfolio.

  • Create a no stick shovel.

  • Buy an easy to read rain gage.

  • Rubbermaid containers can be great alternate if you do not have much yard space.

  • Place a diaper at the bottom of your pot to avoid water from running out the bottom of the pot.

  • Use vegetable cooking oil to fertilize plants.

  • Use citrus peels start your seedlings.

  • Line your indoor pots with coffee filters.

  • Get your gardening started early by planting seeds in eggshells kept inside until proper outdoor weather arrives.

  • Make a mini greenhouse by using recycle plastic bottles
