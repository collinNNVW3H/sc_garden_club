About the Simpson Garden Club

Welcome to the Simpson College Gardening Club. This club is for any students who have a passion for gardening or would like to learn more about gardening.

We meet a couple times per week, with some sessions in the classroom and some session in the garden. If you are interested in signing up please contact

John Smith, club president, at 123-456-7890 or by email at SmithJohnSC@Simpson.edu

Hours and Location

Hours vary by day. Please see our Calendar for further details.

The location of our class changes depending on the type of session scheduled for that day. Classroom sessions are held in the Carver Science Center room 205.

The gardening sessions are held in the Carver Science Center upstairs greenhouse.
